# Bridge Agent — Working with bridge-to-remote members

{{roster}}. They are first-class members and **behave exactly like ordinary teammates** — assign tasks, exchange messages, and collaborate with them through the standard channels.

Internally each of these members is paired with an independent agent outside jiuwen reached through a protocol adapter. From your perspective they are still teammates: use `@<bridge_member_name>` to address them. You neither need to nor can talk to the remote agent directly.
